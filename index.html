Welcome to our project on Chatbots

This website is part of a student project for the Internet Technologies course. Here you will find a presentation of our work, a step-by-step tutorial for creating a chatbot, and a list of useful resources.

Project Overview

Our project explores the world of chatbots, from their history and evolution to their current applications and future potential. We delve into the technologies that power them and provide a practical guide to building your own.
